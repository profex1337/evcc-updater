Pi-Tool is an unofficial tool for evcc: it updates and installs evcc on your Raspberry Pi – or optionally the whole Pi (all packages) – over SSH, with a single tap. Not affiliated with the evcc project.

Features:
* Update evcc (apt) with a before/after version diff
* Dry-run probe – safely check whether an update is available
* Test connection – verify host/port/user/password in seconds
* Install evcc – adds the official apt repo and installs evcc
* Maintenance: restart the evcc service, reboot the Pi, view/share status & logs
* Full system upgrade (optional) – updates ALL packages on the Pi, not just evcc (apt full-upgrade)
* Quick link to the evcc web UI

Security & privacy:
* Credentials stay encrypted on device (Android Keystore)
* SSH host-key verification (TOFU) – protects against MITM
* Optional app lock via biometrics/PIN
* SSH key auth as an alternative to passwords
* No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no cloud

Note: unofficial community tool, not affiliated with the evcc project. For day-to-day charging control there is the official evcc app.

Disclaimer: the app runs sudo commands (apt, systemctl, reboot) on your device. Use at your own risk – no liability for damage to system, data or hardware (MIT license, "AS IS").

Requirement: a Raspberry Pi (or Debian/Linux device) reachable over SSH.
